SIU ICT Fest 2025

17th - 21st March 2025

SIU ICT Fest 2025: a conduit for collaboration

SIU ICT Fest 2025 showcases the tech innovation, expertise, and talent that exists across the nation and champions the vibrant technological ecosystem that spans the UK.

The UK is full of dynamic tech events and gatherings that spotlight the pioneering individuals, initiatives and organisations driving their respective regions forward. UK Tech Week brings these regional successes to a national stage.

The SIU ICT Fest 2025 featured over twenty tech weeks and digital festivals, supported by numerous national advocacy organizations.
